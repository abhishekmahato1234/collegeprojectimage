Contact & Location

Meghauli Serai, A Taj Safari Chitwan National Park is located at the edge of Chitwan National Park in the Terai lowlands of Nepal. Our resort is easily accessed from Kathmandu, both by road (176 kms) or by flight. Transportation is available to and from the international and domestic airports in air-conditioned SUVs or minivans operated by skilled drivers. Please note that all road transfers can be booked on request with Taj Safaris or through your preferred Nepalese ground handler.
